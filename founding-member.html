FAQ

Questions, answered.

What exactly is Panix?
Panix is a marketplace for AI agent skills — like an app store, but for the things agents can do. You subscribe once and access sellers' skills through your own agents.
Is the $199 price going to change?
No. If you're a Founding Member, your $199 covers lifetime Gold access. There are no hidden fees, no upsells, no renewals. You own it.
What if Panix doesn't launch?
Full refund, no questions asked. We're building in public. If we don't ship, you don't pay.
What does "fair-use clause" mean?
It means you use it like a reasonable human. NotAutomated bulk reselling, not running a public API on top of it. Everything else is fine.
When does Panix launch?
We're building toward a Phase 0 launch in Q2 2026. Founding Members get access first — before the public, before the waitlist.
Can I gift a Founding Membership?
Not yet — but we'll add gift transfers after launch. For now, the account holder is the member.
What if I already have a Gold subscription?
You can still become a Founding Member. We'll prorate your existing subscription against the $199. Reach out after purchase.
